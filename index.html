About Me

After more than a decade of corporate experience working alongside C-level executives, last year I decided to follow my passion and begin my journey into a career in web development. In the past year I've been immersed in all things code, attending two coding bootcamps and building dynamic web apps for local businesses. Now I'm ready to begin making meaningful contributions to a development team!

What I lack in experience I make up for with enthusiasm, determination, and endless curiosity. My experience working with technology teams at tech companies like Orbitz.com inspired me to follow my passions. I'm a confident, creative and analytical problem solver who thrives in fast-paced, deadline-driven environments. My extensive experience in project management, combined with excellent communication skills and an inherently flexible nature, makes me a natural fit with any team.

I'm seeking an opportunity that will allow me to apply my existing skillset while continuing to learn and grow my web development knowledgebase.
