About Me

After more than a decade of corporate experience working alongside C-level executives, last year I decided to follow my passion and begin my journey into a career in web development. In the past year I've been immersed in all things code, attending two coding bootcamps and building dynamic web apps for local businesses. Now I'm ready to begin making meaningful contributions to a professional development team!

I'm a driven web developer who thrives in fast-paced, agile environments. I leverages technical and analytical aptitude with excellent communication skills to solve problems quickly and creatively, with intense attention to the details. I am known for learning quickly and effectively juggling multiple deadline-driven projects with ease.

I'm seeking an opportunity as a web developer where I can make immediate contributions, applying my extensive project management experience to work with a passionate development team and create high quality, high-impact web apps.
